# Drop Biscuits
- Servings: 4 large biscuits
- Dish Type: side

## Ingredients
### Dairy & Eggs
- 1 stick _**frozen**_ unsalted butter
- 0.75 cups whole milk
### Pantry & Dry Goods
- 190 grams flour
- 4 grams kosher salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions
1. Preheat oven to 400 F.
2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
3. Grate frozen butter and mix with the flour mixture.
4. Add milk and mix until mixture is barely together.
5. Bake for 15-20 min, allowing more time for larger biscuits.
