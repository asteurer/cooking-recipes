# Sweet Tea
- Servings: makes roughly 0.5 gallons
- Dish Type: beverage

## Ingredients
### Other
- 6 black tea bags
### Pantry & Dry Goods
- 0.5 cups sugar
- 0.125 teaspoons kosher salt
- 0.125 teaspoons baking soda

## Directions
1. Boil 2 cups of water
2. Mix baking soda and water and steep tea bags for 15 minutes
3. Remove tea bags, mix with sugar, salt, and 6 cups of water
