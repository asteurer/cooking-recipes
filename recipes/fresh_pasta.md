# Fresh Pasta
- Servings: 0
- Dish Type: main

## Ingredients
### Dairy & Eggs
- 175 grams eggs

### Pantry & Dry Goods
- 330 grams flour
- 25 grams olive oil
- 10 grams salt

## Special Equipment
- pasta maker
- rolling pin
- pasta drying rack

## Directions
1. Mix ingredients and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
2. Use the rolling pin and pasta maker to shape the noodles.
3. Dry pasta on drying drying rack.

## Additional Notes
I typically triple the recipe.
