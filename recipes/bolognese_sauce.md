# Bolognese
- Servings: 12-15 adults
- Dish Type: main

## Ingredients
### Dairy & Eggs
- 0.5 cups heavy cream or half-and-half
- 2 Tbsp butter
### Meat & Seafood
- 1 pound ground pork or italian sausage
- 1 pound 80/20 ground beef
### Pantry & Dry Goods
- 2 (40-ounce) jar store-bought marinara
### Produce
- 1 diced yellow onion
- Diced carrots (equal portion to onion)
- Diced celery (equal portion to onion)

## Directions
1. Saute mirepoix
2. Add meat and brown
3. Add sauce and reduce to desired consistency
4. Remove from heat and stir in butter + cream

## Additional Notes
This freezes really well.
