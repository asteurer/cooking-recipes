# Minestrone Soup
- Servings: 4-6 adults
- Dish Type: main

## Ingredients
### Meat & Seafood
- bacon
- 1 lb italian sausage
### Pantry & Dry Goods
- red pepper flakes
- salt
- oregano
- sugar
- 1.5 cups of dried mixed beans (cooked)
- 28oz can diced tomatoes
- small can of tomato sauce
- beef or chicken broth
### Produce
- 0.5 onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 celery stalks
- 2 large carrots
- 2 squash/zucchini

## Directions
1. Brown onion, sausage, bacon, and garlic.
2. Add 4 cups of water and remaining ingredients (_**excluding zucchini/squash**_) and simmer uncovered for 45 minutes.
3. Add zucchini/squash and simmer for 15 more minutes.

## Additional Notes
I like to serve this with rice.
